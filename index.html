Jeremy Would Let Me Drown

Atlanta's Premiere Grunge Tribute Band

Biography

Grunge ain't dead, it just (still) smells funny. Jeremy Would Let Me Drown has rocked stages throughout north Georgia with the timeless songs of esteemed grunge pioneers Pearl Jam, Alice in Chains, and Soundgarden. Whether it's the hits that defined a generation or the hidden gems that'll wow the most die-hard of fans, Jeremy Would Let Me Drown recreates the passion and showmanship of the legends for each performance.
